================================================================================
    TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Model Architecture:  ResNet50 (50-layer deep CNN)
Input Size:          224 × 224 × 3 (RGB images)
Output:              2 classes (DR, No_DR)
Parameters:          ~25 million
Pretrained:          ImageNet weights

Training:
- Optimizer:         Adam
- Learning Rate:     1e-4 (adaptive)
- Batch Size:        32
- Epochs:            50 (with early stopping)
- Loss Function:     Cross-Entropy

Dataset:
- Total Images:      2,607
- Training:          2,076 (79.6%)
- Validation:        531 (20.4%)
- Test:              231 (held out)
- Classes:           Balanced (DR: 1,050, No_DR: 1,026)
